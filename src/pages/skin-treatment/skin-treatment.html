Skin Treatment Plan
1. SSKIN treatment plan very high risk of developing pressure ulceration
Skin Inspection

Inspect the skin to all pressure points/areas on a very regular basis observing for reddening, heat, induration, dryness, friction, discolouration, blistering or abrasion.
The exact time scale for this must be determined by previous inspection, general condition of the patient, activities of the day, patient wishes but should be at least 1-2 hourly.
